#Classification Scheme Profile
Description: Descriptive information for an arrangement or division of objects (3.2.87) into groups based on criteria such as characteristics (3.2.14), which the objects have in common.  A classification scheme may be a taxonomy, a network, an ontology, or any other terminological system. The classification may also be just a list of controlled vocabulary of property words (or terms).
